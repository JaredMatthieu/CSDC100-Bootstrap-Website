Akali

Mastery

I like using her when I know the opposite laner is a weak champion in the laning phase and has no escaping ability. She works well in almost all team composition, although your team will lack in constant damage since you are sacrifing a mid carry for an assassin champion.

She's also difficult to pilot, especially in a team fight. It took me time to get to know her well in PC League since camera control is difficult compared to mobile gaming.
