Sylas

Mastery

The first champion I spent time on learning when I played PC League. He's very rewarding when you know the different ultimate abilities that each champion has. He's a mage champion which has a high skill ceiling while having a skill floor that is accessible. His damage is insane and he has his own way of sustaining himself as well. An all-rounder champion that rewards you for being aggressive and knowledgeable.

The only champion that I managed to get compliments for from my League-addicted friends.
